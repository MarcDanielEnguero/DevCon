About Us

DEVCON is the Philippines’ largest tech community and non-profit org for developers, IT Enthusiasts, and future geeks. We exist to promote collaborative growth and global competence of Filipino developers in the academe and IT industry by providing a unique venue for everyone to Sync, Support, and Succeed.

  • We are known for hosting unconferences for IT students, professionals and educators since 2009 with eleven (11) local chapters that serve as regional community hubs across the country.
  • We’ve organized hackathons and innovation challenges in partnership with organizations, enterprises and government agencies;
  • Since 2013, we’ve been hosting the most sought-after developer conference in the country – DEVCON Summit, featuring the best speakers from fields like web3, software development, AI, and the future of work.
